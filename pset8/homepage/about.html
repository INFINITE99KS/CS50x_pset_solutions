Hey there!

I'm Omar Hegab, a passionate compE student, I started CS50 around a month ago and ever since then it has been smooth-sailing thanks to the efforts of the CS50 staff and the amazing community on reddit.

CS50 was really eye-opening for me, as I wasn't really fortunate enough in college to dabble in Data Structures, Algorithms or really any sort of introduction to CS (yet, that is).

So this summer I decided to take it upon me and finish off CS50 as I had it on my radar for quite the time, but unfortunately due to time-constraints at the time I wasn't able to take it.

Now I only got one week to go alongside my final project, needless to say, this is one of the best, if not the best intro to CS course on the internet, packed to the brim with information, regularly updated and arguably most importantly covers topics that you'll undoubtedly need if you ever pursue a career in CS or CE for that matter.
